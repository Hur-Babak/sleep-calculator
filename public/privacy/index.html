This Privacy Policy explains how CycleBed ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit cyclebed.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

CycleBed is a static calculator. We do not ask for accounts, names, emails, or any personal identifiers in order to use the calculator. All sleep calculations happen locally in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

However, when you visit the Site, the following information may be collected automatically by our hosting provider and third-party services:

2. Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners use cookies and similar storage technologies to:

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

Third-party advertisers operating on the Site may also use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies. We do not have access to or control over these technologies. For more information about Google's privacy practices, see policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

4. Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns. Cloudflare Web Analytics is privacy-friendly and does not use cookies or fingerprint visitors. Learn more at cloudflare.com/web-analytics.

5. Data retention

We do not maintain personal user databases. Aggregated server logs are retained by our hosting provider (Cloudflare) according to their retention policies, typically 30 days or less. Advertising and analytics partners retain data according to their own policies.

The Site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
