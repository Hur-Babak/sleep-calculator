What to write about

  • Feedback or feature requests. Want a power-nap mode? A "shift worker" preset? Tell us.
  • Bug reports. If something looks broken — wrong time output, calculator not loading, layout issue on your device — let us know the browser and device so we can reproduce.
  • Corrections. Spot a factual error in one of our articles? Send a source and we'll review.
  • Partnership and advertising inquiries. Direct ad partnerships, sponsored content (clearly labeled), or content collaborations.
  • Privacy or legal questions. Anything about our Privacy Policy or Terms of Service.

What we can't help with

CycleBed is an informational tool, not a sleep clinic. We can't diagnose, treat, or give personal medical advice. If you're experiencing persistent insomnia, daytime exhaustion, or other sleep-related health concerns, please consult a licensed healthcare provider.

Response time

We're a small project. Most emails get answered within 2–3 business days. Complex inquiries — partnerships, legal — may take up to a week.
